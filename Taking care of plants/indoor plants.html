Dwarf Sansevieria Snake Plant , also known as the Snake Plant or Mother-In-Law's Tongue is one of the most popular and hardy species of houseplants. An architectural species, it features stiff leaves that range from six inches to eight feet tall, depending on the variety.

Sansevieria bacularis Mikado is a compact hybrid Snake Plant. Sansevieria bacularis has dark green leaves with slightly paler light green striping. The name bacularis stems from the latin word baculum meaning stick, rod or staff. The Mikado form adds an interesting fountain-like shape look for a more modern look.
